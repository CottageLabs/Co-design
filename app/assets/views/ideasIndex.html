This page lists all the ideas that were considered by the co-designers. These ideas were generated by Jisc, RLUK, RUGIT, SCONUL and UCISA in response to the sector concerns that they have identified during the work of the sector. Only a small number of ideas can be funded through the co-design process but we are making all of the ideas considered public so that we can track and manage them in case we want to revisit any of them in the future.
